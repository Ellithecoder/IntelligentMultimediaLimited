WHO WE ARE

Intelligent Multimedia Limited (IML), is Jamaica’s largest Indoor Digital Advertising Network which reaches over 1+ million viewers monthly, based on impressions. In addition, IML recently launched its newest digital asset with a (36’) high-definition Outdoor video board in the heart of Jamaica’s financial and entertainment district, Knutsford Blvd.; targeting approximately 500,000 viewers monthly.

At IML, we operate a cutting-edge digital signage network that helps brands enhance visibility and engagement through Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) advertising.

BENEFITS of DOOH

Unparalleled Visibility

Our DOOH networks span high-traffic locations such as government departments, urban centers, and commercial spaces. By integrating your advertising messages into these strategic areas, your business can reach a wide range of audiences, from professionals to everyday commuters, maximizing your brand's exposure.

Real-Time Flexibility

With dynamic content management systems, your business can instantly adjust its messaging in response to emerging trends and market updates.

Enhanced Public Engagement

DOOH signage allows your business to utilize immersive visuals and dynamic storytelling to capture the public's attention. With content that educates and informs, your business can effectively communicate its key services to drive greater awareness and understanding among its target audience.

Measurable Impact

The success of your advertising can be tracked using advanced data analytics, providing insights into audience reach, impressions, and engagement.

What We Do

In today’s rapidly moving world, connecting with consumers on the go is essential, and digital out-of-home (DOOH) advertising is the ideal solution. IML uses strategically placed dynamic digital screens in high-traffic areas to deliver compelling advertising that captures the attention of audiences while they are on the move.

Audience

DOOH media can be targeted by market and demographic, allowing brands to pinpoint accurately who they need to reach, then reach them consistently.

Location

As location infiltrates all media formats, DOOH remains the most established location-based expert. DOOH’s targeted locations allow Brands to maximize on the recency of messaging – prompting Consumer Purchases in that moment. Location is considered the best indicator of consumer intent.

Viewability

DOOH is 100% viewable. Our inventory reaches consumers in the real world and is never susceptible to ad blocking. Ensure your brand’s story is seen in an impactful way.
